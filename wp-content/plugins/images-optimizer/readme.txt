<h3>What it does</h3>

Automates the a/b testing process of two Featured Images for posts, pages or WooCommerce products. Our algorithm maximizes clicks on your site through continual testing, saving you time and making you money.

Ideas are suggested based on your article titles, to help you find new things to test!

== Installation ==

<strong>The installation process is very simple:</strong>

1. Install Image Optimizer plugin from wordpress plugin directory or upload the plugin manually to your server at /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through 'Plugins' menu in the Wordpress Administrator.
3. The plugin will be displayed just below your Featured Image section on the right of your posts, pages and products. Simply, upload two Featured Images instead of one.

== Instructions ==
1. The plugin will work on post, pages and WooCommerce products.

2. After activating the plugin go to the post add/edit page.

3. Scroll down and you will se a section titled **Image Optimizer**.

4. Check the Activate Box to enable the plugin to work for the post.

5. Upload First Image.

6. Upload Second Image.

7. Check the reset button to reset the data of that post.

8. Under the Statistic heading you will see the number of clicks and page views.
